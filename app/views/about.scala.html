About Chinese Checkers

A short introduction to our project.

A web app written in Java using Play and Akka Frameworks

Chinese Checkers is a project written by Maciej Kabała and Paweł Narolski for the Coding Technologies (Technologie Programowania) course by Jakub Lemiesz, PhD at Wrocław University of Science and Technology.

We've tried to take a step further from what was required at the course and we've decided to write the project using technologies which were new to us at the start of the project: Play and Akka.

Ultimately we're proud to say that we've managed to accomplish our task and we've had a lot of fun working on this project. It is very stable thanks to a solid Akka framework, and the flexibility of Play proved to be very beneficial late in the coding process.
